Recent Work

The Effect of COVID-19 and the Significance of Vaccination

The goal of this project is to assess the impact of COVID-19 globally, with a specific focus on Portugal, and to investigate the role of vaccination in reducing COVID-19 deaths. To achieve this goal, the project will analyze data on COVID-19 cases and deaths worldwide, and in Portugal specifically, over time. The project will also analyze data on vaccination rates in Portugal and investigate the relationship between vaccination and COVID-19 deaths.

Evolution of Netflix contents (2016-2021)

Netflix is an American subscription video on-demand streaming service and production company. The main goal is to find out how Netflix’s content changed over time (2016-2021) and what content Netflix has available based on rating and category.
